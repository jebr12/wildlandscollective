Take a Break and Get Away from it All

The World is Filled with Noise - Make Room for Silence.

No words needed - we know how you feel. The world is filled with noise. You should make room for silence every once in awhile to keep the batteries charged, to keep the imagination and mind awake. Luckily, that's what we at Wildlands Collective are hear for.

In the wordless short-clip below, produced by the brilliant folks over at Arc'Teryx (and the great filmmakers at Duct Tape Then Beer), trailrunner Adam Campbell chases passion rather than the modern world's trappings. His freedom and salvation very well could have been shot out of the backdoor of any one of our destinations.
