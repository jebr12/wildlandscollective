WHAT IS THE WILDLANDS COLLECTIVE?

Have you ever dreamed about owning your own remote mountain ranch or your own desolately-empty coastal retreat and filling it with the most special people in your life? Have you ever felt the bonhomie and camaraderie of a mountain base camp, a remote surf break, or a jungle outpost – as if everybody present has stumbled upon a magical moment at the same time -- and only wished that fleeting moment lasted longer? Have you yearned for those moments of feeling alive, of being awake and fully present, ever since, and been searching to find it?

The thrill of the wilderness and the necessity of the cubicle and our busy urban lifestyles no longer have to be mutually exclusive.

This is Wildlands. We believe the greatest moments in life involve three simple things:

1) Wilderness: The mystery and magic of unadulterated, wild nature
2) Adventure: Being active, curious, and exploring one’s surroundings
3) Community: Connecting in a deeply authentic way with likeminded individuals -- those individuals who may not look or act alike, but who share the same values, passions, and interests.

“I went to the woods to live deliberately, to front only the essential facts of life and see if I could not learn, and not, when it came to die, discover I had not lived."

Wildlands has scoured the farthest reaches of the world to find that rare breed of off-the-beaten-path lodge that maintains authenticity, end-of-the-world remoteness, light touch, and common meeting spaces to nurture these three simple things -- all while not forgoing authenticity and the spirit of conservation. The Wildlands network of properties reflect the ideals of a base camp—a place where people can come together to share stories and exchange tips before heading out on their life-affirming adventure—all while getting a comfortable night’s rest and not keeping retirement accounts intact.

Wildlands has compiled a world-class collection of remote lodges in iconic wilderness destinations. Think of these lodges as the world's greatest network of wilderness base camps, all with the basic comforts of your favorite hotel. Meant to get you into the heart of the wilderness, to share your adventures around a roaring fire, and to do it all over again. Our experiences seek to be heart-stirring and life-affirming.

While Wildlands calls itself a ‘destination club’, it is far from the pretentious, overglossed time shares of old. It is a movement and a lifestyle. Wilderness and community are its bedrock. We recognize that our busy lives dictate convenience, yet we deeply believe that a wilderness lodging experience need not cost an arm and a leg nor represent a “once in a lifetime” opportunity. For the adventurous lover of the outdoors, for whom “going on vacation” means getting out there, being active, and being inspired, Wildlands seeks to offer a breath and richness of wilderness destinations and experiences that such a traveler need not look anywhere else.

“Do not under estimate the power of a small group of kindred spirits to inspire and change the world”

PAGE TITLE

But why are we a lodging company? Isn’t there a paradox between hotels and wilderness? Usually, yes! While a number of well-recognized adventure and nature-based tour operators exist, the nature of “tour-based” travel removes much of the ‘magic’ that comes from travel and exploring the outdoors – spontaneity, the freedom of do-it-yourself, autonomy, flexibility, etc.

Wildlands believes that the small-scale, remote lodge -- when done well – represents a valiant art. That a minimalist lodge with all the basic comforts that modernity has bestowed upon us in the heart of the world’s wildest landscapes, offers the best of both civilization and the wilderness. There’s nothing like a roaring fire to bring people together – Wildlands’ lodges serve as catalysts to bring the tribe of adventurers, explorers, soul-searchers, and romantics together into a profound community.

Our lodges serve not only as incredible base camps from which to launch life-affirming adventures, but also a mechanism to connect a community of likeminded individuals in uniquely intimate way. The properties and lodges form the heart of Wildlands, but the experience and the people that are part of it are its soul.

“There is a restless wanderer in all of us, and wild, remote places serve as our closest setting to home”

PAGE TITLE

While Wildlands’ adventures might appear to be 100% self-indulgent fun to the outsider, for many of us we do indeed have something else going on. It’s about feeling more alive and more aware to be able to tap into our human potential. It’s not what it seems.

A lot more goes on here than just making powder turns.Wildlands believes that when we lose ourselves in the rugged, wild landscapes that stir our hearts, we allow ourselves to be most alive, most full of potential, and most fully connected -- connected with ourselves, with those around us, and with the environment on which we depend.

Wildlands seeks to be a metaphor for the greatest things in life. Be a part of the Wildlands movement.
