PRESENTING A NEW WAY TO TRAVEL

Wildlands is an online discovery/booking platform and a traveler's cooperative for those that seek out the road-less-traveled and for those who cringe at the way modern travel has become mass-produced and commoditized. Premised around a world-class network of authentic, remote destinations and a likminded community of explorers, we seek to intimately connect a passionate but fragmented community of adventurous travelers with those places and experiences they most crave — many of which remain 'hidden.' We're handcrafting and curating a collection of extraordinary properties and partners — lodges, inns, cabins, huts, ranches, eco-camps, sailboats, and homestays — in the world's most off-the-beaten-path places. Each of our offerings is unique — different in location, look, mood and activities offered — yet all offer a consistent experience that enlivens the explorer's spirit and incubates the formation of a meaningful, offline, and modern community of individuals with shared values and interests.

The seeds of our product come from the spirit of the legendary backpacker trails of yesteryear, where every dusty road was a new possibility, a new dream. We're complemeting that spirit with a beautiful and inspirational product — and above all else, a convenient one — so as to find that perfect balance between adventure, spontaneity, authenticity, and pracitcality. The magic of wild places and the romance of the road no longer need be mutually exlusive from the modern necessity of our busy urban lifestyles.

Think of us as the premier 'destination club' for the adventure and experiential travel industry. We offer a consistent, branded, curated experience across a portfolio of world-class destinations aligned perfectly for the traveler looking to get off the beaten track. Lounge chairs and mega resorts won't be found here. We're all about small being beatuiful, conserving the character and community of a place, and finding authentic, intimate destinations where magic lurks beneath the surface. We'll push you to get out there and explore and feel more connected to the world around you.

“I went to the woods to live deliberately, to front only the essential facts of life and see if I could not learn, and not, when it came to die, discover I had not lived."

A COLLECTION OF REMOTE PROPERTIES AND EXPERIENCES LIKE NO OTHER

The Wildlands network of properties, dwellings, and experiences reflects the ideals of a base camp — a place where people can come together in an intimate setting to share stories and conversation before heading out on a shared adventure. Think of these destinations as the world's greatest network of 'base camps,' — places that are meant to get you into the heart of a wild place, while preseving the basic and essential comforts of a cozy cabin. Minimalist luxury that strives to provide just the right amount of comfort to get your batteries charged, but not so much so that it distracts you from the true luxury of place - the landscape. Gather around the fire with family, friends, and kindred spirits. Feast on a home-cooked meal. Smile and laugh and talk about the day's adventures. Plot tomorrow's discoveries. Our experiences seek to be heart-stirring and life-affirming.

We don't believe in 'off-the-beaten-path' just for the sake of it. Rather, we're confident the untrodden path represents the most meaningful and transformative medium for the traveler - by leaving the city behind (and at times the digital world), the traveler's canvas becomes most-effectively primed. We'll let you do the painting.

“To see the world, things dangerous to come to, to draw closer, to find each other and to feel — that is the purpose of life"

AN INNOVATIVE TRAVEL COOPERATIVE AND 'SIGNATURE EXPERIENCES'

In creating Wildlands Collective, we not only wanted to rethink off-the-beaten-path travel, we also wanted to redesign how we share experiences, interact as fellow travelers, and express our values with our wallets. We realized we wanted to rationalize a very fragmented and difficult-to-plan-and-book segment of the travel world, but we also wanted to inspire a movement for change, and for community. We wanted to create a travel company where the traveler has a real voice in shaping the direction we take, and has ownership in the future of our comapny not just as a customer, but a valued stakeholder. After all, we were founded by the collective comments, frustrations, and dreams of veteran travelers, and we recognized that the ideas and involvement of the crowd will only take a good idea and make it greater.

We also believe in the power of community to enrich all of our lives. Through our collection of 'signature experiences', we bring together a group of likeminded explorers who are eager to expand the boundaries of the human experience and for whom active travel and exploration is not something they do, but part of who they are. Travelers who are endlessly curious and who are looking not just to check off a box on the travel list, but seek to dig deep and feel the true essence of a place. Travelers who are looking for personal awakening, a truly authentic experience, and shared discovery. We realize this type of travel is unique and isn't for everbody, but for those who seek such a travel experience, magic awaits...

While we don't want to force the concept of membership on any of our customers, and we provide axess to all of our properties to non-members alike, if the notion of being part of a membership community excites you, we want to warmly invite you to join our cooperative as a 'founding member.' Doing so will provide you with a members-only discount on all of our properties and experiences, and will also give you priority in all of our signature experiences before we open them to the general public.

“Do not under estimate the power of a small group of kindred spirits to inspire and change the world”

OUR VALUE PROPOSITION TO YOU

Convenience: Put simply, we scour the world to curate a portfolio of the world's most transformative and authentic travel experiences so that you don't have to. We disover and select that rare breed of off-the-beaten-path destination that balances authenticity, comfort, intimacy, and low impact. Travel design, curation, planning, are our passions, and as such, we excel at them. The magic of wild places and the romance of the road no longer need to be mutually exlusive from the modern necessity of our busy urban lifestyles.

Value: Through our innovative cooperative structure, our power comes with numbers. We are able to arrange destination partnerships and design small group experiences that we as individuals counldn't do on our own. That saves us all money. Likewise, we guarantee the lowest price available for the destination when booking with us. Our sustainability as a company should be premised on cutomer satisfaction, and so we think the margins we make as a company should come from repeat purchases rather than one-off transactions. We don't just think this is a better way of doing business, we also believe it holds us accountable in always making our portfolio of destinations and experiences even more transformative, year upon year, so that we only grow more meaningful and insipratonal with time. We're not scared of commitment — we see this as a lifetime relationship, and we take it very seriously to provide our members with value that justifies a lifetime of loyalty.

Access & Community: We're not exclusive for exclusivity's sake, but our collective arrangement provides us with unique access to destinations, experiences, and local influencers — it's the power of the crowd. And no, it doesn't come with the stuffiness of your grandparent's country club or the inauthenticity of your parents' packaged tour operator. In addition, there's a fragmented tribe of likeminded adventurers and explorers scattered throughout the world who share similar passions, personalities and values. The problem is that no mechanism exists to bring us together in meaningful, repeatable offline intereactions. We're building the richest community of travel veterans, weekend warriors, and adventurous spirits, because we all know it's not just about the destination, but who else we suround ourselves with once we arrive. In many of our offerings, we seek to balance the freedom and spontaneity of independent traveler with the social benefits of a group trip.

“There is a restless wanderer in all of us, and wild, remote places serve as our closest setting to home”
