PRESENTING A NEW WAY TO TRAVEL

Wildlands Collective is an adventure travel destination club, cooperative, and membership community, premised around a world-class network of authentic and transformative travel experiences and a likminded community of kindred spirits. Far from a tour operator, we seek to intimately connect a passionate but fragmented community of travelers and explorers with those destinations and travel experiences they most crave, many of which remain 'hidden.'

We're handcrafting and curating a collection of extraordinary destinations, local partners, and dwellings — lodges, camps, ranches, sailboats, and homestays — in the world's most transformative and wild, off-the-beaten-path places. Each of our offerings is unique — different in location, look, mood and activities offered — yet all offer a consistent experience that enlivens the explorer's spirit and incubates the formation of a meaningful, offline, and modern community of individuals with shared values and interests.

And after all, we believe in the power of community to enrich all of our lives. We bring together a group of explorers who are eager to expand the boundaries of the human experience and for whom active travel and exploration in not something they do, but part of who they are.Travelers who are endlessly curious and who are looking not just to check off a box on the list, but seek to dig deep and feel the true essence of a place. Travelers who are looking for personal awakening, a truly authentic experience, and shared discovery. We realize this type of travel is unique and isn't for everbody, but for those that it is, magic awaits...

“I went to the woods to live deliberately, to front only the essential facts of life and see if I could not learn, and not, when it came to die, discover I had not lived."

A DESTINATION CLUB LIKE NO OTHER

The Wildlands network of properties, dwellings, and experiences reflects the ideals of a base camp — a place where people can come together in an intimate setting to share stories and conversation before heading out on a shared adventure. Think of these destinations as the world's greatest network of 'base camps,' — places that are meant to get you into the heart of the wilderness while preseving the basic comforts of a cozy cabin and keeping your savings accounts in tact. Gather around the fire with your new friend. Feast on a home-cooked meal. Smile and laugh and talk about the day's adventures. Plot tomorrow's discoveries. Our experiences seek to be heart-stirring and life-affirming.

While we are a ‘destination club’, we are far from the pretentious, overglossed 'time shares' that the word connotes. For the adventurous lover of the outdoors, for whom 'going on vacation' means 'getting out there' and exploring, we seek to provide a diversity, breadth, and richness of destinations and experiences that our members - each of whom is a modern adventurer and explorer at heart - need not look elsewhere for inspiration and fulfillment.

Think of us as the Club Med of the adventure and experiential travel industry. Only that our sole commonality is in offering a consistent, branded experience across a portfolio of destinations. Lounge chairs and mega resorts won't be found here. We're all about small being beatuiful, conserving the character and community of a place, and finding authentic, intimate destinations where magic lurks beneath the surface. We'll push you to get out there and explore and feel more connected to the world around you.

“To see the world, things dangerous to come to, to draw closer, to find each other and to feel — that is the purpose of life"

PRIMARY MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

Convenience: Put simply, we scour the world to curate a portfolio of the world's most transformative and authentic travel experiences, so that you don't have to. We disover and select that rare breed of off-the-beaten-path destination that balances authenticity, comfort, intimacy, and low impact. Travel design, curation, planning, are our passions, and as such, we excel at them.

Value: Through our innovative collective membership model, our power comes with numbers.We are able to arrange destination partnerships and design small group experiences that we all as individuals counldn't do on our own. That saves all of us money. If you are an avid traveler, if you fail to generate in savings the annual price of membership, either we have failed you, or you joined the wrong club. More importantly, we believe our sustainability as a company should be premised on cutomer satisfaction, and so we believe the margins that we make as a company should come from repeat purchases, via membership renewal, rather than one-off transactions. We don't just think this a better way of doing business, but we also believe it holds us accountable in always making our portfolio of destinations and experiences even more transformative, year after year, so that we only grow more meaningful, and more insipratonal with time. We look at our collective members as a lifetime relationship, and we take it very seriously to provide our members with value that justifies a lifetime of loyalty.

Access: We are not exclusive for exclusivity's sake, but our collective arrangement provides us with unique access to destinations, experiences, and local influencers. It's the power of the crowd. And no, it doesn't come with the stuffiness of your grandparent's country club or the inauthenticity of your parents' packaged tour operator.

Community: We are part of a fragmented tribe of likeminded adventurers and explorers scattered throughout the world who share similar passions, personalities and values. The problem is that no mechanism exists to bring us together in meaningful, offline intereactions that can be repeated. We're building the richest community of travel afficionados, weekend warriors, and adventurous spirits, because we all know it's not just about the destination, but who else we suround ourselves with once we arrive.

Ancillary Benefits: A high-touch travel concierge service that not only ensures that each of our experiences occurs flawlessly and seamlessly, but that works with a landscape of world-class travel partners to offer convenience and value for all your more mundane travel-related needs, that may not necessarily be "off the beaten path." A world-class travel publication meant to inspire your next jaunt. Bespoke travel services for special occasions. Members-only events, lectures, discussions, and screenings. Our benefits don't cease when you leave the wilderness lodge.

The magic of wild places and the romance of the road no longer need to be mutually exlusive from the modern necessity of our busy urban lifestyles.

“There is a restless wanderer in all of us, and wild, remote places serve as our closest setting to home”
