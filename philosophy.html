TRAVEL IS BROKEN

The problem with modern travel is that it's based on going to places to see things. With a destination to destination mentality, our travel experiences become unfulfilling. Little more than ripples on the surface, whereby travel is merely an act of running down a checklist.

What if we looked at it differently? If we put places and things aside and instead slowed down and traveled deeper — traveled based on the experiences and emotions we seek? Traveled with the purpose of being inspired and feeling alive? We think that might very well change the way we interact with the world.

Our reason for being is to create an inspired community of travelers. To use the power of travel to change the world by offering magical, life-altering, transformative travel experiences that highlight the incredible beauty of our world. To empower a network of light-footprint hospitality concepts and a community of adventurers and kindred spirits that see a common bond in each other. We aspire to build a brand that encompasses the inspiration, romance, and excitement of wild places. To do no unnecessary harm. To use business to inspire and implement solutions to the environmental and social challenges of our time.

“Wildlands is more than a collection of lodges, camps and properties - its ethos lies in a community of likeminded travelers who appreciate wild nature and authentic travel."

THE WILDLANDS MANIFESTO

We believe the greatest moments in life involve three simple things:

  1. Wild Nature: Because where it's wild, it's most alive.
  2. Adventure: Getting outside of our comfort zones and squeezing the pulp out of life.
  3. Community: Community is the magic that makes our favorite moments 'pop.' Because the people you travel with often matter more than the places you visit. We believe in connecting in a deeply authentic way and taking part in intimate, meaningful interactions.

We subscribe to the belief that:

  • There is a restless wanderer in all of us, and wild, remote places serve as our souls' true north.
  • Sometimes you have to get lost to be found. The greatest and most rewarding moments in life can be distilled and found in the wild. Wild places help us get back in touch with powerful natural rhythms, help us reflect, and in the process, restore our creativity.
  • To those devoid of imagination, a blank space on the map is a useless waste - to us, where magic awaits
  • Wilderness is not a luxury, but a necessity of the human spirit. Plans to protect ilderness are in fact plans to protect mankind
  • The 'ends of the earth' are as much a mindset as a destination. We'll take you there.
  • We love windy, snowy, wicked days as much as we love a flawless, bluebird day.

“To inspire and to become the brand incarnate of the world's wild places. To facilitate the world's greatest community of active, adventurous travelers who believe in light footprints."

WHY 'WILD'

While Wildlands Collective is a travel company at its core, it sees its role as the facilitator of a community of likeminded individuals who all view what we find in the wild to be much more than self-centered fulfillment. By connecting with the wilderness in a way that’s authentic, we leave feeling recharged, renewed, and inspired. Our decisions in life are more deliberate, and we feel a stronger connection and motivation to conserve and protect the natural world. What's more, by empowering a network of off-the-beaten-path destinations, we believe we are fundamentally activating a more sustainable and community-empowered form of tourism.

Just as Patagonia and the North Face managed to create consumer product gear and apparel brands premised upon exploring the outdoors, so too do we seek to create a travel and hospitality brand that becomes much more than its core offering of travel. Our travel experiences are temporary, but that need not mean they lose value with time. We hope their power and their transformative potential permeate our members' lives for the better, permanently, and allow all of us to open our eyes a bit wider back home.

“There is a restless wanderer in all of us, and wild, remote places serve as our closest setting to home”

SUSTAINABILITY REQUIRES MORE THAN JUST REUSED TOWELS

Wild nature is under increasing pressure from all directions and is rapidly disappearing. We ultimately seek to protect these wild places through the creation of a sustainable travel venture and the formation of a community of likeminded individuals. Wildlands seeks to conserve these places by bringing travelers into these lands and offering authentic, adventurous, inspired and life-affirming experiences and a promise of light footprints. We want to ensure access to these places for us and for future generations, creating a community of like-minded adventurers and stewards of nature, and ensuring their access to these lands now and in the future.

We want the traveler be touched, transformed, and imbued with a passion for stewardship and a catalyst to bring more meaning into the everyday lives of others. We seek to leave little impact but achieve great effect, and empower host communities to authentic destinations to share their love of these wild places.

All business must account for its inevitable environmental costs. What's more, people matter.Locals of the world's wildlands must not be antagonized, but celebrated. We prioritize human resource development, social upliftment, sharing economic opportunities and the encouragement of others to build a better future. As a member of 1% for the planet, we allocate 1% of all revenues to nonprofits working to solve major environmental and huminatarian challenges in the world's wild places. Upon reaching profitability, we will allocate the greater of 10% of our profits or 1% of sales to such causes.
