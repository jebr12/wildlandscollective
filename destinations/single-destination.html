Spend six days in the heart of the Swiss Alps, skiing and snowboarding to your heart’s content. With a trail network spanning over 100 miles, the selection of runs in the Jungfrau region will astound you. Ski through small villages, take in the surrounding beauty, and experience Swiss culture at its finest.

The village of Grindelwald, in the shadows of Eiger, Monch, and the Jungfrau Alps, will provide you with the perfect backdrop for epic riding. Although a popular ski destination, Grindelwald manages to maintain a sense of being a genuine quaint Swiss village amongst its healthy dose of restaurants and bars—providing you with the perfect balance of convenience and escape. Offering plenty of diverse terrain, families and powder hounds alike can discover what makes skiing and snowboarding in the Swiss Alps so magical.

Day 1
Arrival and getting settled

Arrive at Zurich airport, where a tour guide will greet you and help you transfer by train into the Alps. Check into the lodge, and explore the quaint village of Grindelwald, with its Swiss chalets set amongst the majestic Alps. Meet the rest of your group at dinner, and participate in a brief orientation about skiing in the Alps.

Days 2 – 4
Skiing and/or snowboarding

Rise early each morning to experience the best skiing and snowboarding that the Swiss Alps has to offer. Relish in jaw-dropping views of the Alps as you carve turns down the Jungfrau region’s off-piste terrain and untouched snow. Runs include: Gross Scheidegg, Kleine Scheidegg, Mannlichen, and the famous Lauberhorn.Come together each evening to share your adventures over dinner.

Day 5
Zurich

Head out to the slopes for a half-day of skiing, soaking up your last epic turns in the Swiss Alps. If you’re feeling adventurous, there are options for guided backcountry skiing or even heli-skiing to round out your experience. Head back to Zurich in the afternoon, to explore the hustle of bustle of Switzerland’s largest city.

Day 6
Departure day

Check out of your hotel in the morning, say your farewells, and transfer to the airport for your return flight.
