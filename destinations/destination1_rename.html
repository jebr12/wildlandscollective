Spend six days in the heart of the Swiss Alps, skiing and snowboarding to your heart’s content. With a trail network spanning over 100 miles, the selection of runs in the Jungfrau region will astound you. Ski through small villages, take in the surrounding beauty, and experience Swiss culture at its finest.

The village of Grindelwald, in the shadows of Eiger, Monch, and the Jungfrau Alps, will provide you with the perfect backdrop for epic riding. Although a popular ski destination, Grindelwald manages to maintain a sense of being a genuine quaint Swiss village amongst its healthy dose of restaurants and bars—providing you with the perfect balance of convenience and escape. Offering plenty of diverse terrain, families and powder hounds alike can discover what makes skiing and snowboarding in the Swiss Alps so magical.
